The Whisper Skill is designed to provide discreet assistance for commands and queries that are commonly forgotten, such as git commands, terminal commands, jq queries, mongo queries, and more. Whenever you find yourself wondering "how do I do this?" during your daily work, you can ask the Whisper Skill for help without hesitation.
